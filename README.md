# 🌟 Yellow Brigade

> *Bringing essential items, warmth, and most importantly, a smile to people's faces.*

**Yellow Brigade** is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting underserved communities. We believe that small acts of kindness create massive waves of change. Through organized community drives and grassroots outreach, we deliver everyday essentials directly to those who need them most.

---

## 💛 Our Mission

Our work focuses on four core pillars of community support:

*   **👕 Clothing Drives:** Distributing clean, wearable clothes and essential winter/summer wear to underprivileged families.
*   **🍲 Food & Nutrition:** Providing hot meals, dry rations, and healthy food packs to combat hunger.
*   **🥤 Clean Hydration:** Ensuring access to clean drinking water and refreshing drinks during seasonal extremes.
*   **😊 Delivering Smiles:** Organizing community events, educational support, and recreational activities to spread hope and joy.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Features

This repository contains the official frontend website for the Yellow Brigade platform. 

*   **HTML5 & SCSS/Bootstrap:** Built with a clean, fully responsive mobile-first UI wrapper.
*   **Event Tracking:** Includes modern, structured event cards to showcase `Completed` and `Upcoming` community drives.
*   **Optimized Assets:** High-quality visual assets showcasing real-world impact.
